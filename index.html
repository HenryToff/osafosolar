About us

Osafosolar Solutions Engineering is a reputable and fast-growing engineering company based in Accra, with over 10 years of hands-on experience in delivering reliable and innovative solutions in solar energy, CCTV surveillance, Electric fencing and electrical engineering.
The company specializes in the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of solar panel systems, CCTV camera networks, and complete electrical wiring for residential, commercial, and industrial facilities. With a fully equipped shop in Accra, Osafosolar Solutions Engineering is dedicated to providing high-quality products, efficient energy systems, and excellent customer service, ensuring long-lasting performance and client satisfaction.
With our 24/7 online service which provide complehensive support to our client on how they can make engueries and book our due time factor.

Our Aim..

To consistently provide affordable, efficient, and sustainable energy and security solutions by using modern technology, skilled expertise, and a customer-focused approach, while contributing to environmental protection and national development.
